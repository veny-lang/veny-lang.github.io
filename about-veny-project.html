About the Veny Language Project

Veny is an open source programming language designed and developed by Stoyan Petkov, based on over 30 years of professional experience in software engineering and domain modeling.

Veny is currently in an early development stage. The compiler, core language design, and specification are actively being worked on. Project governance, contribution models, and community structure are still evolving.

The vision is to grow Veny into a language supported by an open and collaborative community, with:

  • Clear contributor processes
  • Language roadmap and milestones
  • Community discussion and feedback loops
  • Tooling and platform integrations

If you're interested in contributing or sharing ideas, feel free to star the GitHub repo or follow updates as they come.
